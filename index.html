Explaining AI and what it could mean for you and your business activities

In today's AI revolution, professionals increasingly hear how AI could transform their work - yet the complexity of what AI actually is, coupled with the rapid pace of change can feel overwhelming. At Qi Machine LLC, I bridge this gap by providing focused AI consulting that turns possibility into practical application.

As a US, Maine-based technology consultant with over a decade of software engineering experience in both US and EU markets, I help professionals navigate the AI landscape – by identifying the right tools and explaining their use cases and also addressing crucial concerns about ethics, legality, and limitations. Through structured 90-minute consulting sessions, I demonstrate how AI can enhance your specific workflows while providing clear guidance on implementation, costs, and best practices.

What sets Qi Machine apart is my dual expertise in both AI integration and enterprise software development. This unique perspective allows me to cut through the hype, offering practical solutions that consider your business context, technical constraints, and risk tolerance. While AI consulting is my primary focus, my track record of building production-grade software systems for major corporations ensures you're getting advice grounded in real-world technical experience.

Let me help you transform AI from a looming challenge into a powerful asset for your operations. Learn more about AI consulting services or explore my software project portfolio below to see my technical expertise in action.
