Ignite Sadhana

A transformative journey through guided breathwork

Project Overview

Ignite Sadhana is a comprehensive breathwork application that serves as your companion in the quest for self-discovery and inner peace. The app combines ancient breathing techniques with modern exercise science to deliver a transformative wellness experience, featuring specialized sessions including the world-renowned Wim Hof Method.

Features

The app offers a diverse range of guided breathwork sessions designed to harmonize body, mind, and spirit:

  • Three distinct breathing categories:
    • Upregulating sessions for energy and invigoration
    • Balancing practices for centeredness
    • Downregulating techniques for calm and serenity
  • Exclusive Wim Hof Method sessions
  • Expert-guided recordings for safe practice
  • Mood-based session recommendations
  • Preview of full content catalog
  • Three complimentary guided sessions

Technical Details

Built in native code (SwiftUI for iOS and Kotlin for Android) for a seamless experience, the app features:

  • High-quality audio streaming
  • Secure subscription management
  • Offline session access
  • Cross-platform compatibility
  • Intuitive category-based navigation
  • Integrated payment processing
