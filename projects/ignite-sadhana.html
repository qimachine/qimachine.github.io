Ignite Sadhana

A transformative breathwork app featuring the Wim Hof Method and expert-guided sessions. Upregulate for energy, balance for centeredness, downregulate for calm.

SwiftUI Kotlin Audio In-App Purchases

Project Overview

Ignite Sadhana is a comprehensive breathwork application combining ancient breathing techniques with modern exercise science. The app features specialized sessions including the world-renowned Wim Hof Method, organized into three categories that map to different nervous system states.

Session Categories

  • Upregulating — energy, invigoration, activation
  • Balancing — centeredness, clarity, grounding
  • Downregulating — calm, serenity, deep relaxation

Features

  • Wim Hof Method sessions — exclusive guided sessions for the popular breathing technique
  • Expert-guided recordings — professionally produced audio for safe, effective practice
  • Mood-based recommendations — session suggestions based on how you feel
  • Three complimentary sessions — free access to try before subscribing
  • Offline access — downloaded sessions available without a connection
  • Secure subscription management — in-app purchase integration on both platforms

Technical Implementation

  • iOS: SwiftUI with AVFoundation for high-quality audio streaming
  • Android: Kotlin with ExoPlayer
  • Purchases: Secure in-app purchase and subscription management
  • Storage: Local caching for offline session access
