The Well

Project Overview

The Well is a comprehensive wellness application developed in partnership with Max Wellness, offering both free and premium video courses designed to enhance holistic health. Expert-led instruction spans three core dimensions: Body, Mind, and Being.

Wellness Categories

  • Body — fitness and physical movement instruction
  • Mind — mental health, focus, and cognitive wellness
  • Being — spiritual growth, mindfulness, and self-discovery

Features

  • Free introductory videos — every course has free content to start with
  • Premium content library — full course access via subscription
  • High-quality video streaming — optimized playback on all connection speeds
  • Progress tracking — pick up where you left off across sessions
  • Ad-free experience — clean, distraction-free viewing for subscribers
  • Regular content updates — new courses added on an ongoing basis

Technical Implementation

  • Framework: React Native for consistent iOS and Android experience
  • Video: Efficient streaming and adaptive playback
  • Purchases: Secure in-app subscription management
  • Storage: User progress tracking across sessions
