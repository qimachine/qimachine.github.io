Ayurveda AI

Ayurvedic wellness app with AI-powered constitution analysis and recipe generation.

SwiftUI Kotlin OpenAI Node.js MongoDB

Project Overview

Ayurveda AI helps users discover and understand their unique mind-body constitution through the ancient wisdom of Ayurveda. A comprehensive dosha quiz, personalized recommendations, and a continuously refreshed library of AI-generated recipes guide users toward optimal health and balance in their daily lives.

Features

  • 21-question dosha quiz - detailed analysis of Vata, Pitta, and Kapha elements with a personalized constitution report
  • AI-generated recipes - 10 new dosha-specific recipes added every 24 hours via OpenAI
  • Recipe filtering - browse by dosha type and meal category, save favorites
  • Personalized recommendations - dosha-specific dietary guidance, lifestyle routines, and herb suggestions
  • Seasonal adaptations - wellness guidance that adjusts to the time of year
  • Educational resources - Ayurvedic principles, mindfulness practices, and detailed articles

The Three Doshas

  • Vata - Air and space elements, governing movement and change
  • Pitta - Fire and water elements, governing transformation and metabolism
  • Kapha - Earth and water elements, governing structure and stability

Technical Implementation

  • iOS: SwiftUI, MVVM, local data storage
  • Android: Kotlin, Jetpack Compose
  • Recipe generation: Automated Node.js script calling the OpenAI API on a 24-hour cycle
  • Backend: Express.js REST API, MongoDB for recipe storage and pagination
